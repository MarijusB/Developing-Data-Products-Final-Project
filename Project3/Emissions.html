Introduction

Determining the pollution of countries by measuring the carbon dioxide (\(CO_2\)) emission levels and its relation to countrys standard of living.

A data set contains Gross Domestic Product (GDP), a measure of a countrys economic output that accounts for its number of people (GDP per capita), and carbon dioxide (\(CO_2\)) emissions for 26 countries.

This data set frame contains the following columns:

  • GDP a numeric vector

  • perCapita a numeric vector

  • CO2 a numeric vecto
